and & : all 1 - 1
        any 0 - 0

or | : any 1 - 1
        all 0 - 0

xor ^ : even 1- 0
        odd 1 - 1

negation ~ : flips the bits (0 to 1 and 1 to 0)

>> right shift : a=5(101) a>>1(10) last one bit   
                          disappears
                          a=10(1010) a>>3(0001)
                          >>n = a/2^n
                         
<< left shift : a=5(101) a<<1(01)
                       <<n = a*2^n
